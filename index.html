🔐 Account & Security

Your recovery phrase (also called seed phrase) is the only way to restore your wallet.

- Never share your recovery phrase with anyone.
- This does not offer phone support.
- Support is available only through official channels.

📚 Getting Started

1. Download the official app from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Create a new wallet or import an existing one.
3. Securely back up your recovery phrase offline.

💳 Transactions & Fees

Blockchain transactions require network fees.

. If a transaction is pending, it must be confirmed by the blockchain network. This cannot reverse or cancel confirmed transactions.

📱 Mobile Apps Support

Make sure you are using the latest version of the app.

If experiencing issues:
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Update the app
3. Restart your device

🌐 Troubleshooting

Missing tokens may require manually adding the token contract address.

Delayed transactions are usually due to network congestion.

Advanced Security Tips

🔒 Device Protection

Keep your operating system updated and avoid installing unknown software. Consider using a dedicated device for crypto storage.

🔐 Smart Contract Approvals

Review token approvals regularly and revoke access from unused decentralized applications. Only approve trusted smart contracts.

🌐 Safe DApp Usage

Verify website URLs before connecting your wallet. Disconnect your wallet after completing transactions.

📦 Backup Best Practices

Store multiple offline backups of your recovery phrase in secure locations. Never store it in cloud storage or email.

Additional Wallet Guidance

🌍 Network Compatibility

Always make sure you are sending and receiving assets on the correct blockchain network. Sending tokens to the wrong network may result in permanent loss.

Double-check the network type (ERC20, BEP20, etc.) before confirming transactions.

🔄 Token Swaps

When swapping tokens:

- Review the exchange rate carefully.
- Check slippage tolerance settings.
- Confirm network fees before approval.

Swap prices depend on market liquidity and blockchain conditions.

🛡 Security Best Practices

- Store your recovery phrase offline.
- Avoid public Wi-Fi when making transactions.
- Enable biometric or passcode protection.
- Be cautious of phishing websites and fake support accounts.

📊 Transaction Status

If your transaction is pending:

- It may be due to network congestion.
- Gas fees may affect confirmation speed.
- Confirm the status using a blockchain explorer.

🔐 Wallet Recovery

To restore access on a new device:

- Install the official wallet app.
- Select "Import Wallet".
- Enter your recovery phrase in the correct order.

Never enter your recovery phrase on unknown websites.

⚠️ Scam Awareness

- No legitimate wallet service provides phone-based support.
- Support agents will never request your recovery phrase.
- Ignore messages claiming urgent account suspension.
- Always verify official communication channels.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Why is my transaction still pending?

Transactions may remain pending due to low network fees or blockchain congestion. Confirmation speed depends on network validators, not the wallet application.

❓ Can transactions be reversed?

No. Blockchain transactions are irreversible once confirmed. Always double-check the recipient address before sending funds.

❓ Why is my balance not updating?

Try refreshing the wallet, checking your internet connection, or verifying the transaction status on a blockchain explorer.

❓ What if I lose my recovery phrase?

If you lose your recovery phrase and your device is lost or reset, access to your wallet cannot be restored. Secure backups are essential.

Risk & Responsibility Notice

Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile and involve financial risk. Users are fully responsible for safeguarding their private keys, verifying transaction details, and understanding blockchain network requirements. Digital asset transactions cannot be reversed once confirmed.
